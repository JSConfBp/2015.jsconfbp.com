18 February 2015

Diversity Supporter ticket and Ambassador Program

Diversity Supporter tickets are on sale from today! How does it work:

  • Price is €200 + €100 + VAT + Fees = €400
  • This contains two tickets: one for you, and one for a beneficiary person, whom you support. This person can be a fellow coworker, a meetup member, the only important rule is, that the beneficiary must be from another group
    This includes, but is not limited to: women, people of colour, LGBTQ people, disabled people, and people facing economic or social hardships.
  • The beneficiary will receive the ticket over an email address, which you have to provide on purchase

Our Ambassador Program is for meetup groups and communities:

  • You can apply for the ambassador program here
  • We provide a €50 discount coupon code for your community
  • For every 5 ticket sold with this coupon code, we provide a free ticket for the ambassador who applied for the coupon

Venue

Uránia Cinema in the middle of the city, near the party-district. Designed by Henrik Schmahl in the late 1890's, the interior is characterized by the ornamental motifs of the Venetian Gothic and Moor styles. The place is listed as the world's 3rd most beautiful cinema on Bored Panda, and many tech conferences were hosted here recently, like TEDx, and Strech Conference, because of the unique atmosphere.
